Depressive Disorder Prediction

Depression is a complex and often invisible condition that affects how a person thinks, feels, and functions in daily life. Early understanding and awareness can make a crucial difference. This dashboard offers an anonymous and secure way to explore and predict the risk of depressive disorder using a HistGradientBoosting machine learning model. It highlights key contributing factors from real-world data and presents clear, explainable results to support mental health reflection and action.
